Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Lumis AI, we collect information to provide a better experience for our users. This includes:

  • Account Data: Name, email, and password during registration.
  • Business Data: Information about your brand, niche, and knowledge base content.
  • Connected Accounts: Access tokens and metadata from integrated platforms like Instagram and Meta.
  • Usage Data: Interaction logs with our AI assistant to improve performance.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used exclusively to power your AI assistant. We do not sell your data to third parties. We use it to:

  • Generate contextually accurate AI responses.
  • Identify leads (Contact Names/Numbers) from inquiries.
  • Process payments securely through Stripe.
  • Comply with Meta Platform Policies for data handling.

3. Data Security & Storage

We employ industry-standard encryption to protect your data. All sensitive credentials (like Instagram tokens) are encrypted at rest. We retain your data only for as long as your account is active.

4. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time. You can request a full data deletion by contacting our support team or using the account management tools in your dashboard.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, please reach out to legal@lumisai.com.
