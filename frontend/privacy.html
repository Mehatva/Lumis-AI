Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Lumis AI, we collect information to provide a bespoke AI experience for your brand. This includes:

  • Account Data: Professional name, business email, and encrypted credentials.
  • Business Intelligence: Knowledge base content scraped from your website or provided manually.
  • Meta Platform Data: When you connect your Instagram account, we receive your Page ID, Access Tokens, and conversation metadata to power the AI.
  • Conversion Data: Interaction logs and lead information (names/numbers) extracted by the AI.

2. Data Deletion Instructions (Meta Compliance)

Lumis AI respects your right to control your data. As per Meta Platform Policies, we provide multiple ways to request the deletion of your information:

How to request data deletion:

  1. Via Facebook Settings: Go to your Facebook Profile > Settings & Privacy > Settings > Apps and Websites. Find Lumis AI and click "Remove". You can then click "View Details" to send a formal Data Deletion Request to our servers.
  2. Direct Request: You may email us at team.lumisai@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request and purge all associated records within 48 hours.

3. Data Security & Storage

We employ enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for all Meta Access Tokens. Your data is stored on secure, monitored servers and is never sold to third-party advertisers.

4. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our data compliance officer at team.lumisai@gmail.com.
